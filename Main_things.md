### 🏗️ **PHASE 1: CITY CREATION & BASICS**

**1. City Founding**
```
User starts: /createcity
Bot asks: 
├─ City Name? (max 20 chars)
├─ Flag Emoji? 🏴🏳️🚩⚔️
└─ City Type? 
   ├─ 🏰 Military (Attack +10%)
   ├─ 💰 Trade (Income +20%)
   ├─ 🛡️ Fortress (Defense +15%)
   └─ 🌾 Agricultural (Resources +25%)
```

**Starting Resources:**
- 💰 Gold: 1,000
- 🪵 Wood: 500
- ⛏️ Stone: 300
- 🌾 Food: 200
- 👥 Population: 10
- ⭐ City Level: 1

### ⚔️ **PHASE 5: WAR & ATTACK SYSTEM**

**Attack Process:**
```
/attack

Step 1: Select Target City
├─ Shows: Level, Defense, Resources
├─ Spy Report (if you have tower)
└─ Attack Cost: 500 Gold

Step 2: Choose Army
├─ Infantry: 👤👤👤 (100 each)
├─ Archers: 🏹🏹 (150 each)  
├─ Cavalry: 🐎🐎 (200 each)
└─ Total Power Shown

Step 3: Launch Attack
├─ Travel Time: 30 minutes
├─ Defender gets notification
└─ Can prepare defense
```

**Battle Mechanics:**
```
Attack Power = 
(Troops × Weapons Level × City Buffs)

Defense Power = 
(Wall Level × Towers × Defending Troops × City Buffs)

Winner = Higher Power + Random Factor (10%)
```

**Battle Outcomes:**
```
🏆 ATTACKER WINS:
├─ Steal 30% of resources
├─ +100 War Points
├─ Destroy 1 random building (1 level down)
└─ Enemy loses troops

🛡️ DEFENDER WINS:
├─ Keep all resources
├─ +50 War Points
├─ Enemy loses more troops
└─ Defender gains morale boost
```

**Protection System:**
```
New Cities: 72-hour protection shield
After Attack: 6-hour shield
Can Buy: 24-hour peace shield (5,000💰)
```
